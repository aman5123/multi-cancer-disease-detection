ABOUT AI HEALERS

AI at the Frontier of
Cancer Early Detection

AI Healers is an open research platform combining state-of-the-art deep learning models with clinical imaging to democratize cancer screening and support early diagnosis across gastrointestinal, skin, breast, and brain cancers.

Training Datasets

Kvasir-SEG

1,000 gastrointestinal polyp images with pixel-wise segmentation masks from Vestre Viken Health Trust, Norway.

1,000 IMAGES

kvasir-v1

4,000 multi-class gastrointestinal endoscopy images categorized into 8 clinically relevant classes, captured from real-world endoscopic procedures and annotated for medical image analysis tasks.

4,000 IMAGES

ISIC 2020 Challenge Dataset

33,126 dermoscopic images with expert-annotated diagnosis labels across 9 lesion types from the International Skin Imaging Collaboration.

33,126 IMAGES

HAM10000

10,015 multi-source dermatoscopic images of pigmented skin lesions with validated clinical diagnoses.

10,015 IMAGES

CBIS-DDSM (Curated Breast Imaging Subset)

2,478 mammography studies with pixel-level annotations from the Digital Database for Screening Mammography.

2,478 STUDIES

BraTS 2023 (Brain Tumor Segmentation)

7,023 pre-operative multi-site, multi-scanner, multi-parametric MRI scans, the gold standard benchmark for brain tumor segmentation.

7,023 MRI SCANS
• WORKFLOW

How AI Healers Works

1. Image Upload

Upload medical imaging data including volumetric MRIs, Endoscopies, or Dermoscopic photos securely into our processing queue.

2. Deep Learning

Our backend orchestrates advanced state-of-the-art models like Vision Transformers (ViT) and CNNs to classify and segment anomalies at the exact pixel level.

3. Clinical Insights

Receive high-confidence diagnostic likelihood scores within seconds, giving medical practitioners robust second opinions for accurate disease staging.
